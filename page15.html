E N T R Y  N O .  3

"Movie Date turns to movie marathon?"

     From the percent of pressured students, mostly, quit and decided to just end their misery by the means of suicide. In a perspective of an observer, it’s not a suicide but it is a murder. 

     In a world full of imperfections, why are there still people trying to be perfect for themselves? Why do they look so highly towards themself that they can already decide what will happen next? Who are those individuals that they can already judge a student’s future? A movie directed by Rajkumar Hirani, entitled 3 idiots showcased many issues about the corrupted system the world has. 

     The character in the movie ‘3 idiots’, Ranchoddas, said that “It’s not the pressure on the julgar killed him instead the mental pressure for the past four years did.” College has been known to be hard, making the course of engineering as one of the known hardest. So, why rub salt on the wounded skin? Why pressure someone who has been stress all throughout the years?

     Pressure sometimes has also a good benefit, but it only happens occasionally. Pressure can make someone move quicker, think faster than the usual. It can boost someone’s ability because of the tension. These situations are part of the few good results of being pressured. Keep in mind that there are just countable after-effects of this stress. 

     There may be good aftermaths of pressure but the results of it are still dominated with cruel examples. Mr. Joy Lobo is just an example of teens or students who experienced the anxiety of the inequality of the system. He is just a representation of all the students who chose to end their misery for thinking that they were too brainless to continue life. Many youths also face this kind of misery, and why does the higher ups only give sympathy but no action? 

     Many had already died for it. Numerous people already stopped dreaming because of it. There are several situations where the students chose to stop studying and just stayed at home. Isn’t it still obvious? Pressure has a good effect but if one had exceeded on giving one, see the cruelty. See the difference that it made and resulted. Observe how a brilliant and colorful dream turns into an endless black out. 

     Every little thing has its own consequence. Is it really the rope that killed the said student in the movie? Is being choked up and out of breath is the cause of death of million students? Sometimes, it’s not always the student’s fault of being weak at handling emotions maybe because it’s already too much. An observer would definitely think and conclude, it’s not a suicide. It is a murder and the killer is the pressure.
