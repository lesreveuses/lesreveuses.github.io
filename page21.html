Behind the Thing

Right behind the thing is she,
who wears a smile on her face and a grin.
She seeks nothing more than he,
He who have introduced himself as quite clean.

Indeed, behind the thing is he,
Who bathes in the attention he gets from the 'she'.
Never met her physically, nor even ever wished for it,
yes, he lied to the 'she', a tiny bit.

At the end, the she's left hurt and eventually dead,
From behind the thing, words were left unsaid.
Trust no one behind the thing, they said.
At this unfortunate point is where she was led.

Beware of the things, or even the humans behind the thing,
No one knows who manipulates the shiny strings.
Settle for the real, not the one you see on screen,
Anyways, the deal behind the thing is that, no one actually wins.

Demi Demure
