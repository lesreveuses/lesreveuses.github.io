"I Got My First Phone!"

    Oppo is an international brand that assembles phones in China, according to the manufacturer’s box. It is greatly known as the “camera phone” for it captures images quickly and generates it to a high-definition picture. Oppo A3s was released by the month of June in the year of 2018 and can be still seen for sale nationwide. Unfortunately, the only available shades of the said mobile phone are just in bright red and dark purple.   

    Upon receiving the mobile phone, the consumer should be provided with a charger, two small booklets of quick guide and its safety guide purposes, a metal tool wherein it is used to remove your SIM, and lastly a transparent phone case. These accompaniments should also be placed organized in a standard box of the manufacturer. He or she would also notice that the phone displays a super full screen that is 6.2” wide.

    The person who would unlock the phone using the right key in the right, would notice its standard theme but rest assured because one of the features of this phone is letting the owner to choose different themes and wallpaper that one would surely love! This mobile phone also lets the bearer to multi-task two applications at the same time which he or she can check in the settings (Settings>App Split-screen). This mobile phone also has an advantage of moving files to his or her SD Card with just one tap (Settings>Additional Settings>Storage)! It has also system applications like, phone manager where it can help one’s phone to be more protected. In terms of security, this would let the owner to use a more secure keyboard when typing a password. It also prevents one to screenshot on sites that contains private information.

     If he or she is looking for a budget-friendly mobile phone, this said mobile phone would fit the best. For 6,990 pesos, one would already experience a notch display, a dual camera that has a 13 MP + 2 MP in the rear that captures the real color and 8 MP for the front camera wherein it also gives AI selfies. This information can also be seen in the small booklet of quick guide provided inside the mobile phone’s box. Another thing, this mobile phone would also give you the satisfaction to enjoy watching videos for its great sound and volume. 

     Alas, the phone would already consume 8.73 GB of its storage, which would almost be half of the phone’s over-all capacity if the customer would choose to acquire the version which has 2 GB | 16 GB, but nevertheless he or she can still have the choice to buy a memory card that is equal or higher to the its capacity. Another sad thing about the mobile phone is the camera, its front camera has already a filter that some perhaps won’t like while on the contrary its rear camera would absolutely justify their “camera phone” line.
